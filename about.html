About Us

CertSync was born from the frustration of juggling countless licences, permits and tickets across multiple job sites. As former tradies and compliance managers, we knew there had to be a smarter way.

Today, our mission is clear: cut the chaos of credential compliance so nothing slips through the cracks. Whether you're a solo contractor or a national enterprise, CertSync gives you the clarity and control you need.
